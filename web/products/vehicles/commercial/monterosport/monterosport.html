Overview

The new Montero Sport features dynamic and sporty exterior lines, complemented by a stylish and vast interior space that engenders a sense of comfort, purpose and safety. The Montero Sport is a 7-seater SUV with a 3.2-liter common rail direct injection diesel power plant under the hood. It uses the Super Select 4WD system (Mitsubishi's All Wheel Control philosophy, forged through its participation in the Dakar Rally and other contests) which transmits drive torque effectively to all four wheels on all types of surface. The body, frame and driveline not only give Montero Sport outstanding off-road performance, but also allow it to deliver excellent drive dynamics over all surfaces.
