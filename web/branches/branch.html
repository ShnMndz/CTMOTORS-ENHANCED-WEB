Corner Pasong Tamo and Don Bosco Streets, Makati City, Philippines. Citimotors Makati is located at the heart of the Manila Automotive Row. Inaugurated on May 31, 1989, it provides quality sales and servicing with 380 service bays.

LAS PIÑAS OFFICE

Alabang-Zapote Road, Pamplona, Las Pinas City, Philippines


Citimotors Las Pinas is built on an expansive 5,000 sq.m. lot. It offers complete customer service such as sales of vehicle units, parts and service. Citimotors Las Pinas is proud of its multi-level service shop which operates with more than 200 service bays, and is staffed with highly-skilled and competent service personnel and technicians. The service shop is also equipped with the latest and most advanced tools and equipment.
